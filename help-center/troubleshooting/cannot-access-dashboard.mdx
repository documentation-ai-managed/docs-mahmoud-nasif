---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Quick solution if you can't access mahmoud nasif dashboard.
---

## Symptoms

- Dashboard page won't load
- Error: "Access denied" or blank screen

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check account status">
    Ensure your account is verified. Verify via the email sent after sign-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Sometimes cached data causes loading issues. Clear cache and refresh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser">
    If issues persist, open `https://mahmoudnasif.com/dashboard` in Chrome or Firefox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
